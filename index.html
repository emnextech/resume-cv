Frequently Asked Questions

Can I export my CV to different formats?

You can export your completed CV as a Word document (DOCX) file. This format allows you to easily edit your CV in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or any compatible word processor. The DOC format gives you full control to make further edits, adjustments, or additions to your CV after exporting.

Can I customize the colors and fonts?

Absolutely! You can customize your CV with ease. Change colors from 8 professional color schemes, select from multiple font options, and switch between templates instantly. All changes are reflected in real-time, so you can see exactly how your CV will look before exporting.

Is my data stored or saved?

Your privacy is important to us. All data is processed locally in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or save your personal information on our servers. Your CV data remains on your device, giving you complete control over your information.

Do I need to create an account?

No account required! You can start building your CV immediately. Simply fill in your information, customize the design, and export when you're ready. The entire process is free and doesn't require any registration.

Is this service free? Do you accept payments?

Yes, this website is completely free! We do not accept any payments. All features including template selection, customization options, and CV export are available at no cost. You can use all the tools and features without any charges or hidden fees.
